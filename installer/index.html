armori

Unofficial mod of OMORI for Apple Silicon Macs

- By using this software you agree to the MIT-0 License

- Not compatible with oneloader

- armori requires OMORI to be downloaded through Steam

armori will:
* Backup omori saves
* Copy Game Assets/Saves
* Apply fixes
* Replace OMORI.app
* Follow you into your dreams

Launch through Steam as normal after install

To remove armori, uninstall/reinstall OMORI through Steam

This is an experimental mod, do not contact omori for support.
